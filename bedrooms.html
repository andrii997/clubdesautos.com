Bedrooms

More than a Members Club, a destination. A first-class motoring experience offering high end accommodation in a French country retreat

Club des Autos offers the choice of 13 ensuite bedrooms in the Chateau and 4 independent self-catered apartments in the Club’s newly restored Grange.

Each one of these bedrooms have been designed with their own homely comfort, elegantly furnished with antique furniture, paintings, prints and silks.

From your guestroom, you can admire the magnificent views of the gardens surrounding the Chateau and the main courtyard.

Bugatti

The Club is set in a building of historic importance, dating back to the 14th Century with 17th century additions, featuring a 34 metre high tower with outstanding views across the French countryside.
