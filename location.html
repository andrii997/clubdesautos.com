Location

CLUB DES AUTOS
Chateau de Sermet
24 550 Loubéjac
FRANCE

Located in the heart of the Dordogne close to Villefranche-du-Périgord, Club des Autos is accessible by plane, car or train.

By plane

The nearest airport is Bergerac airport with regular flights to and from London and other destinations. You can fly from other airports such as Limoges or Bordeaux, however please note that both airports are 2h30min drive away from the Club.

By car

Parking is available for pre-war cars, please make sure to book a space in the garage in the ‘Bookings’ page. The garages can accommodate up to 90 pre-war cars.
An open car park for modern cars is also available outside the Club.

By train

The nearest train station is Villefranche-du-Périgord, please make sure to call the Club Manager, Rob Nollet on +33 5xxxxxx to ensure collection is arranged from the train station to the Club.

NORTH – Villefranche-du-Périgord (From Cahors or Toulouse)

As you are approaching from Fumel, you will drive through several small villages: CUZORN / SAINT-FRONT-SUR-LEMANCE / SAUVETERRE-LA-LEMANCE.

As you come up to a roundabout, take the first exit towards LOUBEJAC on to the D59. You will pass a train station on your right, continue straight. As you come up to a junction with the railway on your right, please turn left then straightaway right signposted LOUBEJAC / SERMET / LACAZE / LABOUNET / GADET / LES VITTARELLES. Approximately 2km on on this road, you will see a small signpost with SERMET, turn right on to a gravelled drive.
